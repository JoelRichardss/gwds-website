About us

Bear Necessities Cafe, where every meal is made sustainable and built for the community. Founded by Mindy and Mike, it's part of a movement to help combat food waste and nourish those who need it. Bear Necessities Cafe is a nonprofit organization dedicated to using this food and turning it into nourishing meals for the community. we have a "Pay As You Feel" model, where customers can contribute what they can afford for the meals. For those people who aren't able to pay, we still offer them a nutritious meal without stigma or judgment. We believe that everyone deserves access to good food, regardless of their financial situation. We have noticed that there is perfectly good food is being discarded by supermarkets, and food going to and landfill. We are Determined to make a change, We have a volunteer system so that we can make a difference with your support.
